Litle Lemon is owned by two Italian brothers, Mario and Adrian, who moved to the United tates to start their dream business. They infused their heritage into every aspect of Litle Lemon, from the rustic decor reminiscent of a traditional Italian trattoria to the menu featuring authentic family recipes passed down through generations. Mario, the charismatic frontman, greets guests with a warm smile and a hearty "Benvenuti!" while Adrian, the culinary mastermind, orchestrates the kitchen with precision and passion. Together, they've created a bustling neighborhood eatery where every dish tells a story and every customer leaves feeling like part of the family.
